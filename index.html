Flos Cannabis is a Family-operated class-C facility focused on rare top shelf marijuana with a hand crafted touch. Flos brings together over 30 combined years of experience in medicinal cultivation with a state of-the-art operation located in beautiful Kalkaska, Michigan, sprouted from roots laid in Metro Detroit. In latin, Flos signifies the prime state of something, and we take that philosophy further than just the Cannabis. Our promise is to provide the best consumables possible, using only the most mindful teckniques from seed to smoke. Free from pesticides and harsh fertilizer, always grown with organics, Great Lakes water, and love.
